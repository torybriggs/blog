> The best practicable route to Vancouver is via Route 99

A proactive [war plan](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/War_Plan_Red "War Plan Red") conceived by the U.S. government in the 1920s, later approved in 1930. It was based on the assumption that the growing imperial reach of Britain would draw the United States into war. Fascinating.
